About FN Perfumes

At FN Perfumes, we believe that fragrance is a journey, a story told through scent. Founded in 2010, FN Perfumes crafts luxurious, timeless fragrances inspired by tradition and innovation. Our passion for quality and artistry shines through every bottle we create.

From rich attars to delicate floral blends, we use only the finest ingredients sourced ethically from around the world. Each scent is carefully formulated by expert perfumers to evoke emotion and confidence. FN Perfumes is more than a brand — it's a lifestyle dedicated to elegance and sensory delight.

Meet Our Founder

Noor Ahmed, a master perfumer with over 20 years of experience, leads our creative vision. Inspired by his heritage and love of nature, Noor ensures every FN fragrance tells a story worth wearing.
